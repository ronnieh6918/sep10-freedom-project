Security and Investigation


What is Security and Investigation?

Security and investigation have been very important in keeping important confidential information safe from prying eyes. In the field of Security and Investigation, there are many technologies that help private investigators research and face directly with targeted suspects.


What informations did I research in the department of Security and Investigation?

My research on this topic is about finding existing software apps that is available for all platforms to use and access. The point of finding these software apps is so that those who are private investigators can be manuevered with these apps in order to help effectively during their investigations. Part A: Existing Software and Hardware


Software


Nuance Winscribe Dictation: An app that have a speech recognition function that records and writes whatever you say into text. The app also offers much more functions, but the most beneficial would be putting speech to text faster into documentations.

Tracers: An app that offers access to databases that contains public records and private records. This helps with tracing information and identifying for digital footprint of the suspect in question.

Dropbox: An app that offers managing information and acting as an online storing space for private investigators. Sharing files are easy too when providing documentations.


Hardware


Device Cloning: clones data from targeted device as a way of extracting and replicating the information. This allows private investigators with the digital footprint of the targeted device.

GPS tracking device: GPS device is difficult to be visibly seen by the target. The GPS device is used to track abnormal movements and aiding in providing location of suspect to private investigators.

Surveillance Camera: This is important if private investigators plans to strategically view the suspect without having to engage in keeping a watch close distance. This tool is commonly known that works well and is useful for catching criminal footage or monitoring activities.
