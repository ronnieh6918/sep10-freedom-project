Security and Investigation


What is Security and Investigation?

Security and investigation have been very important in keeping important confidential information safe from prying eyes. In the field of Security and Investigation, there are many technologies that help private investigators research and face directly with targeted suspects.


What informations did I research in the department of Security and Investigation?

My research on this topic is about finding existing software apps that is available for all platforms to use and access. The point of finding these software apps is so that those who are private investigators can be manuevered with these apps in order to help effectively during their investigations. Not only have I researched about different software apps that private investigator can use, there are also existing hardware tools that can also help with their investigation when they need to rely more on confronting suspects rather than using software apps that don't help physically.



Part A: Existing Software and Hardware


Software


Nuance Winscribe Dictation: An app that have a speech recognition function that records and writes whatever you say into text. The app also offers much more functions, but the most beneficial would be putting speech to text faster into documentations.

Tracers: An app that offers access to databases that contains public records and private records. This helps with tracing information and identifying for digital footprint of the suspect in question.

Dropbox: An app that offers managing information and acting as an online storing space for private investigators. Sharing files are easy too when providing documentations.


Hardware


Device Cloning: clones data from targeted device as a way of extracting and replicating the information. This allows private investigators with the digital footprint of the targeted device.

GPS tracking device: GPS device is difficult to be visibly seen by the target. The GPS device is used to track abnormal movements and aiding in providing location of suspect to private investigators.

Surveillance Camera: Used to attain footage on following suspect. This is important if private investigators plans to strategically view the suspect without having to engage in keeping a watch close distance.

Technology 1: Digital Notebook

Future ideas:

My first idea would be a notebook that never runs out, like a digital notebook, without internet access, while you can reshape it by turning it into a folder to store paper files.

  • The notebook should be physically small, so it’s essentially easy to carry and lightweight.
  • The good side to this is that people in the field of security and investigation are able to write on their notebooks rather than solely relying on software apps to record their own audio and write for them, in some cases there might be an internet outbreak and so it might be difficult to access software apps that require internet.
  • The notebook is portable and does not require wifi or internet connection.
  • By not requiring wifi, the notebook can be accessed through charging manually.
  • Save trees since technology has advanced and the amount of trees to make paper decreases.
  • The notebooks are easy to transfer data and information as long as you create an account via proof of identity.
  • The notebooks can be styled according to your liking, for example like a regular notebook using a cover which helps with avoiding detection from suspects.
  • The notebook can detect grammatical errors when you’re writing on it. If you hold a button on the side of the notebook, you can reshape the notebook into a folder!
  • Since the notebook might not be the right size, there is also a size preference option, the notebook can shrink using similar functions like turning down the brightness or sound.
